School Teacher

 

Welcome to GLVNSJCDB site.  You have signed in as a schoolteacher.  This account has the following capabilities:

 

·        Able to view all teacher/teacher aid records in your school.

·        Able to look up and update a student record in your school

 

The following contains a list of database tasks that you will do during a school year.

 

  1. At the beginning of school year, make sure to update the database with the actual students in your class. 

 

  1. Use the search form the find student. If you are unable to find a student in school database, notify database administrator by creating a missing student record.

 

  1. For those student who is in your class database but not physically in your class, set the student class and subclass field to empty.

 

  1. At the end of school year, give each student a grade, which can be either Pass, Fail, 1, 2, 3, or 4.
