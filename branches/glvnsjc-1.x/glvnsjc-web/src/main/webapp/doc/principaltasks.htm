School Principal

 

Welcome to GLVNSJCDB site.  You have signed in as a school principal.  Your account has the following capabilities:

 

  • Create teacher record; make sure to activate login capability for those teacher/TA responsible to update the database.

 

  • Able to look up student form other schools and transfer to your school.  If you are unable to find a student, contact Frere.

 

  • Update your school student records

 

The following contains a list of tasks that you will do during a school year.

 

  1. Divide large classes into sub-classes using the customized split class in My Task folder

 

  1. Create new teacher/TA records.  Remove those who are no longer with your school. Assign GL and VN class to each teacher/TA if available.  Activate login capability to those who are responsible to update student information.

 

  1. When a teacher/TA loses the password, you can reset the password.

 

4.      When a teacher/TA cant locate a student using the school database, he/she will create a missing student record.  Your job is to make sure the missing student record is really not in your school database.  This will reduce the workload that database admin needs to do.

 

5.      Finally, encourage teacher to update his/her own class with the database.
